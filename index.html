About David Corbin

I am a student studying computer science. I have been programming and developing responsive web applications for more than 5 years.

Starting with the web languages (PHP, Javascript, HTML, CSS), I have progressed to learning many popular programming languages including C, C++, Java, Python, and Objective-C. Currently, I am working with the functional language Haskell.

When not developing an application or studying, I enjoy playing the trumpet and listening to a wide variety of musical genres. I also tinker with electrical components, microcontrollers including Arduino, and low power computers including Raspberry Pi.
